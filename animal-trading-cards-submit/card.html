North American Beaver

The beaver has scent glands on its underside used to secrete a liquid that covers its fur and makes it waterproof.

  • Scientific Name: Castor canadensis
  • Average Weight: 24 - 71 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 24 years
  • Habitat: Fresh water sources

The North American beaver is located throughout North America and Canada. Beavers eat leaves, roots, tree bark, and other aquatic plants located near the water sources where they live.
